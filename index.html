Frequently asked  questions

How is my data encrypted?

At StashPort, we opted for native AES-256 ZFS encryption. This means in the case of a physical breach, your data will be unreadable.  

What is StashPort's private cloud?

At StashPort, we offer the ability for our customers to build their own cloud with dedicated server resources and customize it any way they so desire.

Are there mobile/desktop apps?

StashPort at its core, uses a open-source software called Nextcloud. This software also includes apps that allow you to integrate your devices with StashPort. You may also connect via webdav.

How can I upgrade my plan?

By heading over to your billing, you may see an option to upgrade your existing plan.
